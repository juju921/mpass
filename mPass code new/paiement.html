How does the billing work for a trial membership?

All our sites allow for the purchase of a trial membership at a special-offer price and are only available for a certain number of minutes or days. As explained on the join page, all trial memberships are automatically upgraded to standard monthly/bi monthly memberships unless they are cancelled within the trial period.

How often will I be charged for my membership?

Monthly memberships are billed upon sign-up and every 30 days thereafter until cancelled. Trial memberships are billed upon sign-up and are automatically upgraded to standard monthly memberships unless cancelled within the trial period. If a trial membership becomes upgraded, the resulting standard monthly membership billing policy will apply as of the upgrade date.


How do I cancel my membership?

You can instantly cancel your membership online, but you first need to determine which payment processor you used to subscribe to our website. We use several third party payment processors to accept payments for our websites. In order to determine which processor you used to subscribe to the website please check the confirmation email that you received from the processor. This email also contains a link to the online cancelation form, or you can just reply to the email and tell them you want to cancel your subscription.

How can I confirm that my membership is cancelled?

When you cancel your membership, a confirmation e-mail is automatically sent to the e-mail that you used when you subscribed to the webiste. In some cases web-based e-mail providers, such as Hotmail, Gmail or Yahoo, occasionally mistake confirmation e-mails as spam or junk. so please be sure to check the spam/junk folders in order to ensure that the confirmation e-mail was indeed received. If it still appears as though you have never received a membership cancellation e-mail, it might be because the e-mail address was mistyped upon sign-up. In this case please contact the payment processor that you used to subscribe to the website.

Why am I still being charged after I cancelled?

If you have received an e-mail confirmation confirming the cancellation of your membership yet are still being charged, please contact the billing company that you used to subscribe to the website so that they can more closely investigate the matter. Under normal circumstances, a membership cancellation automatically prevents any further billing. You might be a member of more than one of our websites, so please contact the billing company that you used to subscribe to the website.
